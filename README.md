# Netflix Content Insights Dashboard

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard that analyzes Netflix movies and TV shows to uncover patterns in content growth, genre distribution, geographic production, and key contributors in the entertainment industry.

The dashboard aims to provide meaningful insights into how Netflix content has evolved over time and to support data-driven decision making related to media production and platform strategy.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

This project was developed to achieve the following objectives:

1. Analyze Netflix content growth trends over time.
2. Compare the proportion of Movies versus TV Shows available on the platform.
3. Identify dominant genres within Netflix content.
4. Examine which countries contribute the most content to Netflix.
5. Identify top directors contributing to Netflix titles.
6. Identify actors with the highest frequency of appearances.
7. Visualize global content distribution using geographic mapping.

---

## 💡 Benefits of the Dashboard

The dashboard provides the following benefits:

1. Enables quick understanding of Netflix content expansion patterns.
2. Helps stakeholders evaluate content composition strategy (Movies vs TV Shows).
3. Supports genre investment decisions based on popularity.
4. Assists market expansion analysis through country-level insights.
5. Identifies key creative contributors for partnership opportunities.
6. Reveals talent concentration patterns across regions.
7. Provides geographic awareness of content distribution globally.

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations Explanation

### 1. KPI Overview (Total Titles, Movies, TV Shows)

**Purpose:**  
Provides a high-level summary of total content and its composition.

**Insight:**  
Movies represent approximately 70% of the catalog, indicating heavier investment in film content.

**Value:**  
Supports executive-level understanding of platform structure.

---

### 2. Growth Over Time

**Purpose:**  
Shows how Netflix content has expanded annually.

**Insight:**  
Content growth accelerated significantly after 2016, peaking around 2019–2020.

**Value:**  
Demonstrates the impact of Netflix’s global expansion strategy.

---

### 3. Genre Distribution

**Purpose:**  
Identifies the most dominant genres on the platform.

**Insight:**  
Drama, Documentary, and Comedy dominate content availability.

**Value:**  
Helps guide genre investment and production planning.

---

### 4. Top Countries by Number of Titles

**Purpose:**  
Analyzes content production by country.

**Insight:**  
United States and India lead production significantly.

**Value:**  
Supports regional market strategy analysis.

---

### 5. Top 10 Directors on Netflix

**Purpose:**  
Highlights directors with the highest number of titles.

**Insight:**  
Certain directors maintain repeated collaborations with Netflix.

**Value:**  
Indicates partnership opportunities with proven creators.

---

### 6. Top Actors on Netflix

**Purpose:**  
Shows actors with the highest appearance frequency.

**Insight:**  
Actors from India dominate due to strong regional content production.

**Value:**  
Reveals talent concentration and regional industry strength.

---

### 7. Netflix Content by Country (Map)

**Purpose:**  
Visualizes global distribution of Netflix titles geographically.

**Insight:**  
Content production is concentrated in North America, Europe, and India.

**Value:**  
Identifies underrepresented markets for potential expansion.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Netflix experienced rapid content growth after 2016.
- Movies dominate the platform compared to TV shows.
- Drama and documentary genres are highly prevalent.
- The United States and India are the largest content producers.
- Strong collaboration networks exist among creators.
- Geographic distribution remains uneven across regions.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

1. Increase investment in TV series to improve long-term user retention.
2. Expand production in emerging markets such as Southeast Asia and Africa.
3. Maintain partnerships with high-performing directors and actors.
4. Continue investing in dominant genres while exploring niche categories.
5. Monitor content growth to ensure quality control.
6. Strengthen regional content strategies to capture local audiences.
7. Use geographic insights to prioritize market expansion initiatives.
